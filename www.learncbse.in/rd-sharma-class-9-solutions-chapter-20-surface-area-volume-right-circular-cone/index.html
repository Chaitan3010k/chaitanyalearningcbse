RD Sharma Class 9 Solutions Surface Area and Volume of A Right Circular Cone

Summary:

  1. A Right circular cone is a solid generated by revolving a line segment which passes through a fixed point and which makes a constant angle with fixed line. The fixed point is called the vertex of the cone, the fixed line is called the axis of the cone.
  2. For a circular cone of base radius r, slant height l and height h, we have
    • Curved surface area =πrl
    • Total surface area = πr(l+r)
    • Volume = \(\frac { 1 }{ 3 } (area of the base)\times height\)
