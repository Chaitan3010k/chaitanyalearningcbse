Haryana Scholarship 2021 | Scholarship for SC, ST, OBC, Eligibility, Rewards, Application Process and Documents

June 3, 2021 by Bhagya

Haryana Scholarship 2021-2022: Haryana Scholarship is an initiative of the Welfare of Scheduled Caste & Backward Classes Department, Government of Haryana. This scholarship offered to all meritorious students of the affiliated colleges of the state every year. This scholarship was initiated with the aim of encouraging SC/ST/Backward class students of the state for pursuing education. This scholarship offers financial support to the SC/ST/OBC/ Minority and merit students of Haryana state. Haryana Scholarship online form 2021-2022 last date is in between January and February 2021.

The Govt of Haryana has introduced many scholarships for improving and promoting education in Haryana. So, all the eligible students can apply online for Haryana Scholarship on its official website on or before the due date. Read the complete article to get all the information about the Haryana Welfare Scholarship Online Form. Haryana Scholarship for General, SC, ST, OBC 2021.

You can also find more Scholarship Articles for 12th passed, 10th passed Students and many more.

Haryana Scholarship can be applied through online mode only. The Welfare of Scheduled Caste & Backward Classes Department implements many scholarships for the welfare of SC and Backward Classes in the state. This department works to coordinate and supervise the activities of other departments to implement many schemes. These schemes are designed for the welfare of SC, Backward Classes, and Denotified Tribes.

The Govt of Haryana completely committed to bringing out welfare schemes to SC, Denotified Tribes, and Backward Classes of the society. The state Govt with its various subsidiary departments provides many scholarships for the financial help of deserving students. These scholarships offered by the Directorate of Elementary Education, Department of Higher Education, Haryana State Council for Science & Technology and many more. Students who want to apply for Haryana Scholarship can refer to the below-listed scholarship schemes.

Haryana Scholarship List

Some of the scholarships are listed for Haryana scholarship for students:

  • Dr. Ambedkar Medhavi Chhatra Yojana 2021
  • Haryana Post-Matric Scholarship for OBC Students
  • Haryana Post-Matric Scholarship for SC Students
  • Haryana POSE Scholarship

  • HSCST Fellowship for Ph.D.
  • Financial Assistance to SC/OBC Students for Higher Competitive Entrance Examinations
  • Haryana State Merit Scholarship

Refer below the description of some of the popular scholarships.

Dr. Ambedkar Medhavi Chhatra Yojana 2021: This scholarship has implemented by the Welfare of Scheduled Caste & Backward Classes Department, Haryana. This scholarship provides monthly financial aid to the meritorious students of the SC and Backward Classes of the state.

Haryana Post-Matric Scholarship for OBC Students: This scholarship offered to the OBC students pursuing post-matric classes under Govt of India. This scholarship amount ranges from Rs. 160 to Rs. 750 per month in Post- Matric to Post-Graduate classes. However, this scheme has a family income criteria. The family income of OBC students should not be more than Rs. 1 lakh per annum.

Haryana Post-Matric Scholarship for SC Students: This scholarship offered to the SC students pursuing post-matric classes under Govt of India. The amount of scholarship varies from Rs. 230 to Rs. 1,200 per month in Post- Matric to Post-Graduate classes. However, this scheme has a family income criteria. The family income of SC students should not be more than Rs. 2.50 lakh per annum.

Haryana POSE Scholarship

This scholarship was initiated by the Haryana State Council for Science and Technology (HSCST). This scholarship will be awarded to 150 students. Haryana Promotion of Science Education (POSE) scholarship offered to the B.Sc. and M.Sc. students for promoting science education. This scholarship offered to Haryana students who have chosen basic and natural science subjects in the following courses:

  • 3-year Bachelor in Science (B.Sc.)
  • 4-year BS
  • 5-year Integrated M.Sc./MS
  • 2-year Master of Science (M.Sc.)

Haryana NMMS Scholarship

National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship (NMMS Scholarship) is a centrally sponsored scholarship. This scholarship offers financial assistance to meritorious students of economically weaker sections. So that they can continue their studies after class 8th. Students pursuing class 8th can apply for this scholarship. Students have to appear in MAT (Mental Ability Test) and the SAT (Scholastic Aptitude Test) test to get selected for this scholarship.

Haryana NTSE Scholarship

Students pursuing class 10th can apply for the NTSE Haryana scholarship. National Talent Search Examination (NTSE) conducted in 2 stages. Students who will clear the Haryana NTSE will have to appear in NTSE stage2 conducted by NCERT. Haryana NTSE conducted on November 2021.

HSCST Fellowship for Ph.D.

The Haryana State Council for Science and Technology (HSCST) offers to Haryana state students. Those who want to pursue a Ph.D. from any recognized university of the state. This scholarship offers research fellowships to students who have chosen CSIR-JRF. But, students must have qualified at least CSIR-LS NET.

Haryana State Merit Scholarship

This is a merit-based scholarship. This scholarship offers financial support to students belonging to economically weaker sections. This scholarship helps students for pursuing studies after class 12th up to the post-graduation level. Students who have taken admission through a competitive exam. The exam should be conducted by the Punjab or Kurukshetra university can apply. Otherwise, students who have taken admission on merit basis can also apply for this scholarship.

Haryana Scholarship Eligibility Criteria

Students must fulfill the following eligibility criteria to apply for Haryana Scholarship:

  • The student must be a domicile of Haryana state.
  • Students belonging to minority communities can apply.
  • The family income of students should be below 2.5 Lakh per annum.
  • Students pursuing an undergraduate or postgraduate degree from a recognized institute can apply.
  • Students must have secured at least 60% attendance in the class.

How To Apply for Haryana Scholarship?

Refer to the below points to for Haryana Scholarship Online Apply.

  • Go to the official website of the Haryana Scholarship Portal http://haryanascbc.gov.in/
  • Click on the ‘Application Forms’ appeared under the ‘E-Services’ menu on the homepage.
  • The list of Haryana Scholarship forms will be displayed on your screen http://haryanascbc.gov.in/application-forms
  • Click on the required scholarship application form link to download the form.
  • Download the Haryana Scholarship application form in pdf format.
  • Then, read all the prescribed instructions to fill the Haryana Scholarship application form.
  • Fill the correct and necessary information in the Haryana Scholarship application form.
  • Attach all the supporting documents along with the Haryana Scholarship application form.
  • Submit the scholarship application along with the supporting documents to the Head of the institution.

Documents Required for Haryana Scholarship

Attach the below-listed supporting documents to apply for Haryana Scholarship.

  • Attested copies of the previous examination certificates and mark sheets
  • Attested copy of domicile certificate
  • Attested copy of ration card
  • Attested copies of caste/tribes and income certificate
  • Photocopy of income declaration
  • Hostel certificate from the concerned warden (only for SC candidates)
  • Photocopy of the first page of bank passbook in the name of the student
  • Attested copy of the receipt of tuition fee/other charges
