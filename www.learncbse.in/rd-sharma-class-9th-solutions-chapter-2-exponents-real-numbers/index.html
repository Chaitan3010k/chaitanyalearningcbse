Question 1.
The value of {2 – 3 (2 – 3)3}3 is
(a) 5
(b) 125
(c) \(\frac { 1 }{ 5 }\)
(d) -125
Solution:
{2 – 3 (2 – 3)3}3 = {2 – 3 (-1)3}3
= {2 – 3 x (-1)}3
= (2 + 3)3 = (5)3
= 125    (b)

Question 2.
The value of x – yx-y when x = 2 and y = -2 is
(a) 18
(b) -18
(c) 14
(d) -14
Solution:
x = 2, y = -2
x-yx-y = 2 – (-2)2 – (-2)
= 2 – (-2)2 + 2 = 2 – (-2)4
= 2 – (+16) = 2 – 16 = -14        (d)
