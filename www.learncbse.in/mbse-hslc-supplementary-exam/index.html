Mizoram Board HSLC Supplementary Exam 2019 | Results (Released), Time Table, Admit Card,

June 26, 2019 by Bhagya

MBSE HSLC Compartment/Supplementary Exam 2019: The Mizoram Board of School Education (MBSE), established in 1975, is an autonomous governmental body for academic administration in Mizoram, India. It has the power to regulate, supervise and control school education in Mizoram. Its primary function is to prepare academic programs and organize examinations, especially for state-level High School Leaving Certificate (HSLC) and Higher Secondary School Leaving Certificate (HSSLC). From 2012, the board also conducts State Technical Entrance Examination (STEE) for entry into technical courses such as engineering, medicine, veterinary science, pharmacy, nursing, homeopathy, and dentistry.

MBSE HSLC Compartmental Exam Result 2019 has been released on 26th June 2019.

You will be getting a second chance in the form of a supplementary exam, to be held in the month of June 2019 (tentatively). You need to be armed with all the latest updates regarding MBSE HSLC Supplementary exam 2019 to do well, which is why we have compiled this article here. How To Download MBSE HSLC Supplementary Exam Time Table 2019?

To make full use of the time available to you, it is necessary that you know the exam time table. Follow the below steps to download it from the official site:

  1. Visit the official MBSE website (link given above).
  2. At the top, click on the drop-down menu “Examination” and then choose routines → High school.
  3. Click on the “MBSE HSLC Compartment/Supplementary Time table 2019” link.
  4. The time table opens as a pdf.
  5. Download and keep it safe. Take a print out for convenience.

Steps to Apply MBSE HSLC Compartment/Supplementary Application Form 2019

  1. Visit the official MBSE website (link given above).
  2. Under the notification tab, look for the “MBSE HSLC Compartment/Supplementary Exam application form 2019” link.
  3. Clicking on it will open a new page.
  4. Register using your roll number and other personal details.
  5. Finish the payment process after filling the form to complete the registration process.
  6. Take a print out of the application form for future reference.

MBSE HSLC Compartment/Supplementary Admit Card 2019

The admit card cannot be obtained online and will be provided only in offline mode. The school authorities will provide it to the students along with the official signature and stamp. Take xerox copies of the same and keep it safe, as you won’t be allowed to write the exam without your admit card. If you notice any discrepancies/errors in your admit card details, notify the concerned official immediately.

MBSE HSLC Compartment/Supplementary Result 2019

The results will be released in online mode on the official website in the month of July (tentatively). Follow the below steps to view your results.

  1. Visit the official MBSE website (link given above).
  2. At the top of the page, there is a “Results” option. Click on it.
  3. A new page will open. Click on the “MBSE HSLC Compartment/Supplementary Exam Results 2019” link.
  4. Enter your roll number and registration number.
  5. Click on “view results”.
  6. Your results will be displayed in a new window.
  7. Download and take a print out of the same for future references.
