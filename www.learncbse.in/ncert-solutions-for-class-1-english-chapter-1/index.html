NCERT Solutions for Class 1 English Chapter 1 A Happy Child

Summary In English
This is the poem of a child who lives in a little red-coloured house. The child always remains happy. She laughs and play for the whole day. She never cries. There is a green tree near the child’s house which saves the child from the heat of the sun and the child sits under this green tree after she finishes her play.
