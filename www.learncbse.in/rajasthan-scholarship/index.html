Rajasthan Scholarship 2021-22 | Complete List, Eligibility, Award Details

June 9, 2021 by Bhagya

Rajasthan Scholarship 2021-2022: Rajasthan Scholarship is also known as SJE Scholarship. Rajasthan Scholarship is Applicable exclusively to Rajasthan State students, the Rajasthan Government with its subsidiary departments offers scholarships under various schemes of Rajasthan Scholarship to meritorious, disadvantaged and deserving state students. The state scholarship allows graduates to pursue higher ednauucation in the system with as many as nine institutions, more than 250 colleges, 55000 primary schools, and 7400 high schools. The state also has more than 40 colleges of engineering. Read on to find more about Scholarship in Rajasthan.

Rajasthan scholarships are discussed in detail in this article. It covers answers to all your questions related to state-run scholarships, including an all-inclusive list of Scholarship Rajasthan along with all the details you need. Rajasthan Scholarship 2021-22 last date is different for different scholarships under Scholarship Portal Rajasthan.

The application deadline for Post matric scholarships for the academic year 2020-21 for class 11 and 12 students in Rajasthan has been extended till 31st March 2021.

When are you allowed to apply for them? Get the response in the table below to all these questions. It contains a full list of all the scholarships available to Rajasthan students.

Rajasthan Scholarship Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicants for the scholarship must be 10th and 12th with at least 60 percent marks from the recognized board.
  • While applying for a scholarship, the student should be 17 years of age.
  • Candidates should only be in the following categories: SC, ST, EBC, SBC, OBC Candidates must be Rajasthan State residents.
  • For OBC candidates, the maximum annual family income of the category SC/ST/EBC/SBC should be Rs. 2.50 lakh and Rs. 1 lakh.
  • Candidates should have all the valid Rajasthan scholarship documents required.

Rajasthan Scholarship Application Process

  • First, you need to visit Rajasthan Scholarship Portal.
  • Candidates Click the Student Option Then read all the instructions on the page.
  • Enter your full name, mobile number, a legitimate e-mail Address (for possible correspondence) and code, etc. in the registration box. All Institute Details Should Correct the Submission Click.
  • You will receive “Account Activation Link” on your email account after completing the registration process, which you mentioned during the registration process.

Documents Required for Rajasthan Scholarship

  • Address Proof Certificate
  • Caste Certificate
  • Income Certificate
  • 10th/12th Class Marksheet
  • Receipt of Fees
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Passbook details, etc

Rajasthan Scholarships Award Details

The government of Rajasthan runs different schemes for the welfare of the backward classes and the SC/ST. It is not necessary to refund financial assistance to an applicant for their higher schooling and scholarship money. The Government of Rajasthan has supported all students from SC/ST/OBC/General/Minority with many scholarship schemes. Those students who are Waiting for Scholarships can now apply/register themselves through the Rajasthan Government official website.

Post-Matric & CM Scholarship, Rajasthan

This scholarship applies to students in the category SC/ST/OBC/EBC/SBC/DNT and studies at a recognized school in class 11 or 12. Supported by the Rajasthan State of Government’s Department for Social Justice and Empowerment, this scholarship seeks to provide financial support for higher education for students belonging to these classes. The grant includes most of the higher education care budget and tuition.

Eligibility Criteria

  • It is possible to apply for candidates from the SC/ST/OBC/SBC/EBC/DNT categories.
  • Students must study in a recognized school in class 11 or 12.
  • Community annual income should not surpass Rs. 2,50,000 (for candidates for SC/ST/SBC), Rs. 1,00,000 (for candidates for OBC/EBC), Rs. 2,00,000 (for candidates for DNT) and Rs. 5,00,000 (for students studying at a nationalized institution).

How to Apply?: Apply online through the new portal of the Rajasthan Government’s Department for Social Justice and Empowerment.

Award Details: Maintenance allowance, study tour cost, mandatory non-refundable fees reimbursement, book allowance, etc.

Dr. Ambedkar Post Matric Scholarship for Students belonging to EBC, Rajasthan

This scheme can benefit economically backward class (EBC) students in pursuing their post-secondary studies. The financial support provided under this scheme includes maintenance allowance, tour fees for studies, reimbursement of compulsory non-refundable fees, book allowance, reader fees (for blind students), and more. Students who fulfill all of the scholarship’s eligibility requirements may apply for it.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Candidates that belong to the general but may contribute to the EBC group.
  • The family’s annual income should not exceed Rs. 1 Lakh.
  • The grant extends to candidates from class 11 to graduation to pursue their studies.

How to Apply?: Apply online via the digital website of the Rajasthan Government Department for Social Justice and Empowerment.

Award Details: Maintenance allowance, research tour cost, required non-refundable fees reimbursement, book allowance, etc.

Post Matric Scholarship for SBC Students, Rajasthan

The state government also provides post-matric scholarships to SBC (Special Backward Class) students who are studying at the post-secondary level, i.e. from grade 11 to PG. The aim of the scholarship is to encourage SBC students to pursue their education after class 10 by providing them with adequate financial support in terms of maintenance allowance, study tour price, reimbursement of mandatory non-refundable charges, book allowance, etc.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Students who belong to the classification of SBC and research at the postgraduate level in class 11 can submit.
  • The family’s annual income should not surpass Rs. 1,08,000.
  • Students who undertake studies can also apply by distance learning/correspondence.

How to Apply?: Apply online via the digital website of the Rajasthan Government Department for Social Justice and Empowerment.

Award Details: Maintenance allowance, research tour cost, compulsory non-refundable fees reimbursement, book allowance, etc.

Ambedkar International Scholarship Scheme for SC Students, Rajasthan

Under this scheme, SC students willing to pursue doctoral studies at a university/institution abroad may have the opportunity to gain the financial support of up to Rs. 25 Lakh. It is applicable in the fields of social science, public administration, law, economics, political science, and anthropology to pursue doctoral studies. Students who fulfill this scholarship’s eligibility requirements will follow their dream research career overseas without financial constraints.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Students in the SC class can apply.
  • Students with a total of 55 percent marks must have finished their postgraduate studies from a recognized institution.
  • Participants should also have been accepted to a Ph.D. program in the specified disciplines at a college abroad–Social Science Public Administration Legal Economics Political Science Anthropology The applicant’s age should be under 35 years.
  • The family’s annual income should not be greater than Rs. 6 Lakh.

How to Apply?: Offline application by Ambedkar Peeth, Mundla, Jamvaramgadh, Jaipur, Secretary. Email Id: [email protected]

Award Details: Rs.15000 a month

Chief Minister’s Higher Education Scholarship Scheme, Rajasthan

Governed by the Department of College Education, Rajasthan’s Chief Minister’s Higher Education Scholarship Program extends for students who have completed their 12th grade with at least 60 percent marks and are on the merit list of the first 1 Lakh students whose annual family income is no more than Rs. 2.50 Lakh. This qualification cum ensures the students receive a scholarship sum of Rs. 5,000 per annum dependent on scholarship.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Students who have completed their 12th class with marks of at least 60 percent and are classified under 1 Lakh student’s merit list may apply.
  • The annual income from the community should not reach Rs. 2,50,000.
  • The applicant should be in a nationalized bank with a clear bank account

How to Apply?: Apply online via the single sign-on portal of Rajasthan

Award Details: Rs. 5000 per month

Rajasthan Yuva Vikas Prerak Internship Program (RYVP)

This internship program is offered by the Rajasthan government to all students who have freshly graduated, postgraduate, or are at the end of their studies. The plan often refers to students currently earning a Ph.D. from an accepted institution. A monthly stipend of Rs. 25,000, as well as a correspondence allowance of Rs. 2,500 a month, will be given to eligible candidates under this scheme.

Eligibility Criteria

  • The applicant must be in the 18 to 35-year age group.
  • The Engineering/Medical/Law/Agriculture graduate students, postgraduates in any field and doctoral students may apply.
  • The candidate should have strong control and solid interpersonal and delivery experience over MS Office.

How to Apply?: Apply online via the Rajasthan Single Sign-On website

Award Details: Rs. 2,500 a month Contact allowance A stipend of Rs. 25,000 per month
