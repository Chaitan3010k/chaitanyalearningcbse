Kerala SSLC Result 2020 | Check Kerala Board 10th Class Result 2020

April 12, 2020 by Bhagya

Kerala SSLC Result 2020: Kerala Board Class 10th Result 2020 will be announced by Kerala State Education Board (KSEB)  on its official website, www.keralaresults.nic.in. The date for the release of results has been released on May 2020.  Students who have given the class 10th exams, for them the results will be declared by the conducting body, very soon.

State Board Results

Candidates who want to check Kerala Board class 10th results 2020 have to enter their Roll Number as mentioned on their Admit Card. The exam for Kerala board SSLC was conducted from 10th March 2020 to 26 March 2020. There is no exact news until now about the result declaration date but it will be announced soon. But as soon as it will be announced by the Kerala government, we will be updating here regarding the same. Till that time students who are waiting desperately for the results, are requested to have patience.

Students, who have appeared for Kerala class 10th exams should calculate approximate marks as per exam, given by them. Take out your questions paper, and start mentioning marks as per the correct answers and do the grand total for each subject. This will help you tally the marks obtained by you in final results. If there is a huge difference between your expected marks and the original marks, they can apply for rechecking/re-evaluation.

Here in this article, we are going to give complete details for results of Kerala class 10th results conducted this year in 2019. Keep reading with us.

Kerala SSLC Result 2020

As per the notification announced by the Government of Kerala, the results for Kerala board SSLC 2020 exams, will be released on May 2020. Let us give here a general description of the Kerala Board 10th exam.

  • Examination Name – Kerala Board SSLC Exam 2020
  • Conducting Body – Kerala State Education Board (KSEB)
  • Examination Mode  – Offline(Pen and Paper)
  • Exam Dates – 10th March to 26th March 2020 To be announced
  • Exam Results – To be announced
  • Official Website – www.dhsekerala.gov.in
  • Result website – www.keralaresults.nic.in

Kerala SSLC Result 2020 – Steps to Check

Kerala Board Result 2020 Class 10th will be declared on the official website and through online mode only. Later, students can collect their SSLC mark sheet from their respective schools as per mentioned by the school. Below here are the steps to check the results for 10th class exam conducted this year in 2020, through online mode.

  • Firstly, the candidate has to visit the official kerala board results website of Kerala Board, www.keralaresults.nic.in
  • Click on the link given for Kerala Board SSLC results in 2020.
  • A new webpage will get opened where you will be asked to enter the roll number and date of birth.
  • Enter the required details and click on submit button.
  • The result of class SSLC Kerala board exam will be displayed on your screen.
  • Download the scorecard for your reference.

Kerala SSLC Result 2020 – ScoreCard

Scorecard of Kerala Board Class 10th results consist of following details

  • Roll Number
  • Name of the Student
  • School Name and Code
  • Father’s and Mother’s name
  • Marks obtained in each subject (Practical and Theory)
  • Total Marks Scored
  • Grade as per marks obtained
  • Result Status(Passed/Failed)

Kerala SSLC Result 2020 Re-evaluation

If the students are not satisfied with their marks or if they have not passed the exam, they can choose for revaluation of their results, declared by the Kerala board. To apply for it, students will have to download the application form for a re-evaluation of marks available in the Kerala Board online website.

Students have to send the filled scrutiny form along with nominal fee deposit fee to the Kerala Board authority. After the submission of the form, the Kerala board authority personnel will recheck the answer sheets for the subject, which has to be re-evaluated.  The cross-checked results will be published again on the official Kerala Board results site.

About KSEB

The Kerala State Education Board (KSEB) was established in the year 1990, located in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. The aim of the Board is to restructure the operations of Secondary and Collegiate Education in Kerala. The main objective is to conduct the board exam for SSLC and DHSE classes and to prescribe syllabus for all the schools, private or government, which are affiliated to the board. The board is responsible to affiliate the private and government schools in Kerala, also.

Kerala board is responsible for the improvement of the secondary and higher secondary education so that it meets the required needs and standards of economic and social development. KSEB provides adequate facilities for the educational and professional advancement of socially and educationally backward communities. Apart from the class 10th and 12th examination, Pareeksha Bhavan also conducts other examinations, for certification of qualified teachers through KTET and other similar examinations.
