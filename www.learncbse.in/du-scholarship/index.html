DU Scholarship 2020 | Dates, Application and Selection Process, Eligibility

February 27, 2020 by Bhagya

DU Scholarship 2020: The DU Scholarship consists of many scholarships, each targeting various sets of students and courses. The scholarship is provided by Delhi University, one of the premier universities in the country. Furthermore, the sheer number of scholarships offered by the university is tremendous. Financial assistance ranges from book grants to full-fledged scholarships. Read on to find more about DU Scholarship for SC Students 2020.

DU Scholarship for UG and PG Students 2020

Following is the complete list of DU scholarships which can be availed under the Delhi University:

  • Undergraduate Scholarships (13 scholarships)
  • Postgraduate Scholarships (2 scholarships)
  • Departmental Scholarships (86 scholarships)

DU Scholarship Eligibility Criteria

  • Students should have cleared their class 12 examinations from CBSE board or equivalent.
  • The candidate must hold only a SINGLE scholarship. If found holding more than one, they can be debarred from the scholarship program.
  • Students pursuing courses from any DU colleges are eligible.

DU Scholarship Selection Process

The scholarships will be disbursed by the Selection Committee on the basis of merit. The members of the Selection Committee includes Deputy Registrar, Deputy Finance Officer and Registrar.

FAQ’s on DU Scholarship

Question 1.
What is the official website for DU Scholarship?

Answer:
Students can access the official DU website at http://du.ac.in/du/index.php?page=opportunities-scholarships.

Question 2.
What is the selection process for the DU scholarships?

Answer:
The scholarships are disbursed by the Selection Committee on the basis of merit. The members of the Selection Committee are the Deputy Registrar, Deputy Finance Officer, and Registrar.
