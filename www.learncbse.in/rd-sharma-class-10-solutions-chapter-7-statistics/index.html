Question 11.
The arithmetic mean of the following data is 14, find the value of k. (C.B.S.E. 2002C)

X 5 10 15 20 25
f 7 k 8 4 5

Solution:
Mean=14

⇒ 14 (24 + k) = 360 + 10k
⇒ 336 + 14k = 360 + 10k
⇒ 14k- 10k- 360 -336 24
⇒ 4k = 24
⇒ k= \(\frac { 24 }{ 4 }\) = 6 4
Hence k = 6

Question 12.
The arithmetic mean of the following data is 25, find the value of k. (C.B.S.E. 2001)

X 5 15 25 35 45
f 3 k 3 6 2

Solution:
Mean =25

⇒ 25 (14 + k) = 390 + 15k
⇒ 350 + 25k= 390 + 15k
⇒ 25k- 15k = 390 -350
⇒ 10k = 40 ⇒ k = \(\frac { 40 }{ 10 }\) = 4
Hence k = 4

Question 13.
If the mean of the following data is 18.75. Find the value of p.

X

 10 15 P 25 30
f 5 10 7 8 2

Solution:

⇒ 460 + 7p = 32 (18.75)
⇒ 460 + 7p = 600
⇒ 7p = 600 – 460 = 140
⇒ p =  \(\frac { 140 }{ 7 }\) = 20
∴ p = 20
