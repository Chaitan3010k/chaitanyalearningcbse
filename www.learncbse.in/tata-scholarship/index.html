Tata Scholarship 2020 | List of Scholarships, Dates, Eligibility, Rewards

February 28, 2020 by Bhagya

Tata Scholarship 2020: Tata scholarship is an excellent opportunity for those well-deserved students who need funding to fund their education. The Tata Group Philanthropy Units, Tata Trusts, Sir Dorabji Tata Trust, Sir Ratan Tata Trust & Allied Trusts, are offering a number of Tata scholarships to promote quality and affordable education not only among brilliant students but also underprivileged students who are unable to afford it. Some of these scholarships include Tata Trusts Career Development Grant/Travel Scholarship, Tata Trusts Means Grant for University, Maharashtra, Tata Housing Scholarships for Meritorious Girl Students and several others. 20 students to be awarded the JN Tata Endowment Scholarship.

Students studying at different levels will benefit from the benefits offered by the Sir Ratan Tata scholarship for engineering students and tata scholarships for medical students. In this article, you will find all the relevant details of a number of Ratan Tata Trust scholarships. Other specifics include a number of  JN Tata scholarship (Cornell university for Indian students), their acceptance duration, prizes, eligibility requirements, application process and more. Please find below the list of scholarships provided by Tata Group non-profit organizations, tata scholarship for masters, tata scholarship for abroad studies, tata scholarship for mbbs students.

Tata Scholarship Eligibility, Award Details

Now Let us find about the details of list os scholarships under the Tata Scholarship Scheme

Tata Trusts Professional Enhancement Grant/Travel Scholarship

  • Objective: One of the most successful Tata scholarships, this scholarship provides financial support to those applicants who wish to study abroad for professional development.
  • Award: Travel award dependent on candidate’s financial needs
  • Eligibility: Indian nationals seeking to travel abroad for technical development
  • Application: Electronic application through the official website of Tata Trusts.

Tata Trusts Means College Scholarship, Maharashtra

  • Objective: This Tata scholarship offered by the Tata trusts gives the monetary support to Maharashtra school students.
  • Award: financial assistance as per student requirements
  • Eligibility: Class 8 to 10 students enrolled in a school located in Mumbai/Mumbai suburban areas
  • Application: electronic application through the official website of Tata Trusts.

Tata Trusts Medical and Healthcare Scholarships

  • Objective: Tata scholarships, Tata Trusts Medical and Healthcare Scholarships provide the required monetary assistance to students who wish to pursue higher education primarily in the fields of medical sciences and health care out of many scholarships.
  • Award: 30-80 percent of the scholarship amount
  • Eligibility: Bachelor’s/Master’s candidates in the medical sciences and health care disciplines.
  • Application: electronic application through the official website of Tata Trusts

Tata Trusts Women Neuroscience Scholarship

  • Objective: Led by Tata Trusts, this Tata scholarship seeks to enable female students to undertake their Neuroscience studies by providing them with monetary support.
  • Award: Tuition fee exclusion and other associated fees paid to the college
  • Eligibility: female students with a ranking of at least 50 percent and able to obtain M.Sc. In Neuroscience
  • Application: Online application via the official website of Tata Trusts.

Tata Trusts Scholarship for Speech Therapy

  • Objective: The main objective of this scholarship is to enable students to obtain a Master’s degree in Speech Therapy by providing financial assistance.
  • Award: Tuition fee waiver and other associated fees paid to the Institute
  • Eligibility: graduate students with a minimum score of 60 percent and able to obtain a Master’s degree in Speech Therapy
  • Application: electronic application through the official website of Tata Trusts

Tata Housing Scholarships for Meritorious Girl Students

  • Objective: This is considered as one of the most popular scholarships for girls. Also, the main objective of this scholarship is to encourage meritorious along with the needy girls to pursue technical education.
  • Award: A scholarship of up to Rs. 60,000
  • Eligibility: Girl students in the 2nd year of BE/B.Tech. (Civil Engineering) or B.Arch., a minimum score of 50 percent in the qualifying test, annual family income not exceeding Rs. 3 Lakh from all sources
  • Application: electronic application through the Vidyasaarthi web portal

J N Tata Endowment Loan Scholarships

  • Objective: This Tata scholarship provides low-interest loans to students wishing to pursue higher education abroad. Under this scheme, the trustee may waive interest if the loan is paid by the students within a specified period of time.
  • Award: Loan grant up to Rs. 10 Lakhs with travel assistance of up to Rs. 50,000 and gift scholarship of up to Rs. 7,50,000.
  • Eligibility: final year undergraduates or graduates with a good academic record; no more than 45 years of age
  • Application: electronic application through the J.N website. Tata Endowment for Indian Higher Education.

Tata Scholarship – Cornell University, NY

  • Objective: Offered by the Tata Education and Development Trust, this grant is one of the most common Tata scholarships for undergraduate students who want to complete their studies at Cornell University, New York. A minimum of 20 students is sponsored each year under this initiative.
  • Award: up to full scholarship rates depending on the financial needs of applicants
  • Eligibility: Indian students accepted to a 4-year undergraduate program at Cornell
  • Application: All UG candidates will register for financial assistance at Cornell University at the time of Cornell’s application for entry.

Tata Trusts D.Ed and B.Ed. Scholarship

  • Objective: One of Tata’s famous scholarships, the Tata Trusts Scholarship for D.Ed. B.Ed. Provides financial support to those graduates who want to obtain D.Ed. Or B.Ed, man.
  • Award: Tuition fee reimbursement and other associated fees paid to the college
  • Eligibility: students from Jammu and Kashmir, Bihar, Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Sikkim, and Tripura, with a minimum score of 60% in the last academic year and ready to undertake D.Ed. B.Ed.
  • Application: Digital application via the official website of the Tata Trusts.

Tata Trusts Means Grant for College

  • Objective: The primary goal of this Tata Scholarship is to give financial assistance to college students to complete their education without worrying about monetary difficulties.
  • Award: financial assistance as per student requirements
  • Eligibility: students of class 11 onwards (excluding engineering courses) and located in Mumbai/Mumbai suburbs
  • Application: electronic application through the official website of Tata Trusts.

Lady Meherbai D Tata Education Scholarship

  • Objective: Initiated by Tata Education Trusts, this scholarship is one of Tata’s most successful scholarships. The goal of this Tata scholarship is to enable female candidates to pursue higher education internationally in the fields of social work, social sciences, education, gender studies, child health, public health, rural development research, business communication, etc.
  • Award: financial assistance
  • Eligibility: woman graduates from renowned universities with an outstanding academic record, minimum work experience of 2 years.

Documents Required for Tata Scholarship

  • Salary slip of candidate’s parent
  • Records of money earned in 2016 and 2017
  • Records of untaxed income and benefits for 2016 and 2017
  • Current bank statements
  • Current mortgage information
  • Records of savings, stocks, bonds, trusts, and other investments
  • The student’s noncustodial parent’s email address, if applicable
  • Income Tax return

FAQ’s on Tata Scholarship 2020

Question 1.
Which is the main scholarship that is offered only to the meritorious girl students?

Answer:
The main scholarship that is offered only to the meritorious girl students is Tata Housing Scholarships for Meritorious Girl Students

Question 2.
Which is the scholarship that helps students wishing to study abroad a loan with a low-interest rate?

Answer:
The scholarship that helps students wishing to study abroad a loan with a low-interest rate is J N Tata Endowment Loan Scholarships.
