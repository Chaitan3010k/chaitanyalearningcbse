Download SSLC Supplementary Exam Time Table 2019

  • Go to the official website for the supplementary time table- kseeb.kar.nic.in
  • Click on the Notification option on the left sidebar and then click on Exams Time Table under the SSLC Exam tab.
  • Students have to click on Time Table for June 2019 to get the Karnataka SSLC Supplementary Time Table.
  • Karnataka SSLC Supplementary Time Table 2019 will appear on the new window.
  • Download the Time Table and keep it for future use.

Karnataka SSLC Supplementary Exam Admit Card

The admit card for SSLC supplementary exam 2019, conducted by Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board, will be released on June 2019, before the start of examinations. The exams are going to be conducted from 21st June to 28th June 2019. The admit card for all the students will be released online on the official website, kseeb.kar.nic.in.

Students who have applied for supplementary exam for class 10th, have to download the admit card from the official website itself. No other mode will be available to get the admit card. Follow the below steps to download it.

  • Go to the official website for SSLC Supplementary Admit Card – kseeb.kar.nic.in
  • Check the link given for admit card in the notifications and click it.
  • Enter your Registration Number and click on Submit.
  • Admit Card will appear on the new screen and students have to check for their mistakes. If any discrepancies exist, they must report to the school authorities immediately.
  • Take a printout of the admit card and keep it safe with you to present in the exam hall.

Karnataka SSLC Supplementary Exam Results

The results for SSLC supplementary exam 2019, conducted by Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board, will be declared on July 2019, after the conduct of examinations. The exams are going to be conducted from 21st June to 28th June 2019. The results for all the students will be released online on the official website, kseeb.kar.nic.in.The level of the exam will be Karnataka State board level.

The official link to check the supplementary exam results is www.karresult.nic.in. Students who want to check the results for SSLC supplementary exam, follow the given steps;

  • Go to the Karnataka SSLC official results website – www.karresult.nic.in
  • Click on the Supplementary SSLC Result 2019 link.
  • Students are required to enter Registration Number and click on Submit.
  • Karnataka SSLC Supplementary Exam 2019 Result will appear on the screen.
  • Download and take a printout of it for future reference.
