KIIT Scholarship 2020 | Dates, Application Process, Benefits

February 27, 2020 by Bhagya

KIIT Scholarship 2020: KIIT Scholarship organized by the Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT), Bhubaneswar, Odisha. This scholarship offered to the meritorious students pursuing their studies in any course at KIIT. KIIT Scholarship encourages the deserving and excellent students to study at KIIT. KIIT is a top institute located at Bhubaneswar, Odisha. This University offers many UG and PG courses such as engineering, management, computer application, law, medicine, fashion, journalism, etc.

KIIT offers several scholarship programs every year under the scholarship schemes. The duration of the scholarships is subject to change from time to time. So, Interested students can apply for a Scholarship that suits their needs and recognition. Read this article below to know the complete information about KIIT Bhubaneswar Scholarship.

KIIT University Bhubaneswar Scholarships 2020

The Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT) is one of the top institutes in Odisha. KIIT was founded in 1992 as a vocational training center with only 2 staff and 12 students. However, it became an institution in 1997, after starting a degree engineering program. KIIT has developed with a strong interdisciplinary culture and a commitment to teaching excellence.

KIIT also set up its Polytechnic and achieved Tier 1 Accreditation by NBA. In 2004, KIIT was declared as Deemed University by the Ministry of HRD, Govt of India. KIIT has provided 4777+ job offers in 114 companies to the KIITians. KIIT offers various scholarships and fellowships to the students studying at KIIT like KIIT Scholarship for Biotechnology Students, KIIT PhD Stipend, KIIT Scholarship for B Tech, KIIT Scholarship for international students. The Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT) offers the following Scholarships to students at the university.

  • Pradyumna Bal Memorial Scholarship
  • Tuition Fee Waiver Scheme
  • KIIT Merit Scholarship
  • KIIT Poverty Cum Merit Scholarship
  • KIITEE Merit Scholarship

Pradyumna Bal Memorial Scholarship

This scholarship was organized in memory of Late Pradyumna Kishore Bal, Founder President of KIIT. KIIT University Scholarship offers free education to two poor and meritorious students every year.

  • Benefits: The selected students will get College Fees and Hostel fees including books, mess, and other ancillary expenses.
  • Selection Process: The scholarship applications received from students will be submitted to the selection committee. The selection committee will shortlist the candidates based on their academic excellence and family income. The decision of the selection committee to shortlist a candidate will be final.

Tuition Fee Waiver Scheme (TFW)

This scheme provides seats up to 5% of the sanctioned B.Tech. courses. The seats provided will be supernumerary in nature and the waiver limited to the tuition fee only. The beneficiary has to pay all other fees except the tuition fees.

KIIT Merit Scholarship

This scholarship offered to the university toppers (1st, 2nd, and 3rd) of each semester for six months.

KIIT Poverty Cum Merit Scholarship

This scholarship has been organized by KIIT for the poor and needy students.

  • Benefits: The scholarship amount and duration will be decided based on the need of the shortlisted candidates.
  • Selection Process: The scholarship applications received from students will be submitted to the selection committee. The selection committee will shortlist the candidates based on their academic excellence and family income. The decision of the selection committee to shortlist a candidate will be final.

KIITEE Merit Scholarship

This scholarship has been organized by KIIT to provide free education to KIITEE-2019 toppers. This scholarship will be provided only to the B.Tech. (4 years) students who have taken admission at KIIT. Also, KIIT provides scholarship benefits to the top 1000 rank holders of KIITEE-2019 who are studying B.Tech. (4 years). This scholarship supports students to fulfill the criteria during his/her study at KIIT.

Benefits: The following benefits will be provided to the selected students under this scholarship.

Ranks Scholarship Amount
General Rank 1 to 1000 Rs. 1,20,000 per annum
Best Ten (top 1 to 10) of Central and State Boards Rs. 1,00,000 per annum
KIITEE Rank 1 Rs. 60,000 per annum

Selection Process: The scholarship applications received from students will be submitted to the selection committee. The selection committee will shortlist the candidates based on their ranks. The decision of the selection committee to shortlist a candidate will be final.

How To Apply for KIIT Scholarship?

Follow the following steps to apply online for KIIT Scholarship:

  • Step – 1: Visit the official website of the KIIT Scholarship, https://kiit.ac.in/
  • Step – 2: Click on Apply now
  • Step – 3: Select the course and click on the confirm button.
  • Step – 4: Before applying read the instructions.
  • Step – 5: Click on APPLY ONLINE.
  • Step – 6: Submit the application by entering all the necessary details.

Documents Required for KITEE Scholarship 2020

  • Education qualification certificates
  • Photograph
  • Signature

KIIT Scholarship Selection Criteria

  • A cut-off qualifying mark will be fixed by the KIIT at the time of the declaration of the Entrance Result.
  • As per the availability of seats in different courses, cut-off Rank for counseling will be notified. Candidates, having a rank above cut-off rank, shall be called for counseling.
  • For B.Tech. 4-year program: it will be on the basis of marks obtained in Mathematics, then in Physics and then by age (preference to older candidates).
