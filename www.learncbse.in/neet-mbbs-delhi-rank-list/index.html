NEET MBBS Delhi Rank List 2019, 2018 | Check Delhi MBBS Merit List Here

July 1, 2019 by Bhagya

NEET MBBS Delhi Rank List 2019: MCC (Medical Counselling Committee) will publish state quota seats for Delhi MBBS Merit List 2019 while Directorate General of Health Services will publish the All India Quota seat on behalf of the MCC. DU MBBS/BDS Rank list will be released in online mode. there are two rounds in NEET Counselling 2019 and the merit lists for both rounds will be declared separately. 85% of seats of state quota will be on the basis of NEET Score of each candidate. The Delhi MBBS Rank List will be released in the last week of June 2019. and for round II it will be released in the third week of July 2019.

NEET Merit List

NEET MBBS Delhi Rank List 2019 for 85% State Quota Seats

Before going into the detailed Delhi MBBS Merit List, it is necessary for you to know about the essentials of NEET MBBS Delhi Rank List 2019:

  • Medical Counselling Committee, Delhi University will release NEET MBBS Delhi rank List 2019.
  • Delhi University will release NEET merit List in the last week of August 2019.
  • DU MBBS Rank List will release separate rank list for state quota (85%) and All India Quota (15%)
  • NEET Cutoff 2019 for state quota seats are released by Medical Counselling Committee, whereas the Directorate General of Health Services on behalf of the MCC will release the All India Quota Seat cutoff.

How To Download NEET MBBS Delhi Rank List 2019?

NEET MBBS Delhi rank List 2019 will be made available for students in PDF format, here are the steps to download NEET MBBS Delhi Rank List 2019:

  • Step – 1: Visit the official website of Medical Counselling Committee @ mcc.nic.in.
  • Step – 2: Click on the link which shows Delhi NEET Rank List 2019
  • Step – 3: On Screen, it displays NEET MBBS Delhi Rank List 2019 in PDF Format.
  • Step – 4: Click Ctrl + F on your keyboard and search for your name or registration number and press enter.
  • Step – 5: Now download NEET MBBS Delhi Rank List PDF and take a print out of it for future reference.
