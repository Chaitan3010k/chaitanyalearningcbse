KVPY Admit Card 2019: Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana (KVPY) is a prestigious and national level scholarship program that is conducted by the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore. The admit card is a very important document without which you will not be allowed to enter the exam hall. It will be released in online mode on the official website. You will be able to download you admit card only after you register for the KVPY Exam. Read on to find out more.

Candidates can check their roll number, Seat number, Exam Center venue and some other details on KVPY Admit Card 2019. How to Download KVPY Admit Card

The admit card can be downloaded from the official site using your credentials. It will be available for viewing and downloading from 12th October till the exam date. Given how important the document is, it is better to take more than one copy of it for safety. From these steps, you can easily get your KVPY admit card 2019 Download process

  1. Visit the official KVPY website.
  2. Click on the “applications” tab on top of the homepage.
  3. Click on “Download admit card”.
  4. A new page will open. Select “login”.
  5. You can now log in using your username and password.
  6. Once you log in, click on “Admit card”.
  7. Select “Assessment” from the drop-down menu.
  8. Click on “Download” to download your admit card.
  9. Take a few print outs of it for future reference.

KVPY Admit Card 2019 – Unable To Download

  • You need to register first to download your admit card. In some cases, you might be unable to access your admit card due to discrepancies in your application form.
  • If that is not the case, there might be some technical issues. Please wait for some time and try again later.

What To Do In Case of Discrepancy In KVPY Admit Card?

If you observe any of the following discrepancies in your admit card, you need to inform the concerned authorities immediately

  1. If your name is misspelled in KVPY Admit Card
  2. If your PwD/DA status is incorrect in KVPY hall ticket
  3. If a different test city is allotted rather than the one filled during KVPY Registration
  4. If your photograph is not visible on admit card
  5. If you are unable to download admit card despite successful registration

KVPY Admit Card – Instructions

You would need to preserve the admit card well after the exam ends and until admissions are over. Hence, it would be wise to keep these instructions in mind:

  1. A candidate must not spoil or mutilate KVPY Admit Card.
  2. Candidates must also not change any entry made therein. For any changes in the information given on the admit card, contact the exam authority to the earliest.
  3. Candidates are required to preserve their KVPY admit cards in good condition until the entire selection process is over.
  4. Do not carry any unfair items.
  5. Any mutilated, altered or invalid admit card will lead to cancellation of your candidature.
  6. Also, carry multiple copies of the KVPY Hall Ticket in the exam hall.

KVPY Exam Center – Items Barred At Exam Center

Take care not to carry the following items with you, lest you be barred from attempting the exam.

  • Any electronic material such as a watch, microphone, Bluetooth, earphone, mobile, health band, calculator, pager, etc. is not allowed inside the exam hall.
  • You are also not allowed to carry pieces/bits of paper, pencil box and the like to the exam hall.
  • It is recommended to wear open footwear not closed shoes.
  • Candidates are also advised not to put Mehendi or any cosmetic color on their hands as it may cause an error while doing digital fingerprint scan recording on the exam day.
  • They cannot sit for the exam unless they clear the fingerprint scanning.

Documents To Be Carried for KVPY Exam

Along with the admit card, you are required to carry the following documents:

  1. Valid ID Proof (School ID, Aadhar ID, Voter ID, etc.)
  2. Caste/Disability certificate if applicable
  3. 2 Passport size photograph.

FAQ’s on KVPY Admit Card

Question 1.
When was the KVPY 2019 admit card released?

Answer:
The KVPY Admit Card was released on 7th October 2019.

Question 2:
How can I download KVPY Hall Ticket?

Answer:
You can download your KVPY admit card 2019 by following the steps mentioned below:

  • Visit the official KVPY website.
  • Click on the Applications tab which is on the top of the home page.
  • Click on the link provided to download the KVPY hall ticket
  • Enter your User ID and Password
  • Download the KVPY admit card

Question 3:
Is there any dress code for the KVPY exam?

Answer:
There is no such dress code for the exam but it is advisable for male candidates to wear light coloured shirt/t-shirt half sleeves, they should not wear kurta or pyjama. Female students should wear light coloured clothes, no flowers, bangles, jewellery except for Mangal Sutra.
