February 28, 2020 by Bhagya

Maharashtra Scholarships 2020: The Government of Maharashtra administers a number of scholarships for the candidates who are a resident of the Maharashtra state. Maharashtra scholarships strive at presenting suitable monetary support to exemplary and underprivileged students belonging to diverse levels of life. Visit the Maharashtra Scholarship website to apply for Maharashtra scholarships.

Maharashtra, having the second-highest literacy rate in the nation, has certainly developed as an education center. It covers 1 central university, 19 state universities and 21 regarded universities that generate a handful of opportunities for candidates living in Maharashtra and those appearing from various corners of the country. Summing to this, the scholarships here act as a driving force in rendering hassle-free way to education to all the candidates of Maharashtra state including candidates belonging to SC, ST, VJNT, OBC, EBC and other backward classes. Maharashtra Scholarship Exam Date 2020 is on 16th February 2020. Maharashtra Scholarship 2020 last date is different for different applications.

Maharashtra Scholarship Eligibility Criteria

In this section, we will discuss the eligibility requirements that students need to meet to apply for the Maharashtra scholarship scheme. The primary eligibility standard that demands all scholarship is that the candidates must be a citizen or permanent resident of Maharashtra state. Moreover, there are many other conditions too, such as students require to satisfy family income limit, educational merit, category, etc. Find out, the eligibility requirements for all Maharashtra scholarship exam date 2020 given below.

Post-Matric Scholarship to SBC candidates, Maharashtra

  • The candidates seeking an education at a post-matriculation level can apply.
  • The candidates, who are interested in full-time employment or are redoing the same class cannot apply for the scholarship.
  • They must secure 75% attendance for the current year

Tuition Fees and Examination Fees to SBC candidates, Maharashtra

  • The scholarship is available for candidates studying at a post-matriculation level in a government or government-aided institution.
  • The yearly income of the family should not exceed INR 8 Lakh.
  • The candidates must have obtained admission through CAP means.

Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Vasatigruh Nirvah Bhatta Yojna, Maharashtra

  • The scholarship is applicable for the wards of registered labor or the child of Alpabhudarak or both.
  • The annual income of the family should not be more than INR 8 Lakh.
  • The candidates must have attained admission in a technical, professional or non-professional program at graduation or postgraduation level.

Tuition Fees and Examination Fees to VJNT candidates, Maharashtra

  • The scholarship is accessible for candidates studying at the post-matriculation level in a government/government-aided institution.
  • The annual income of the family should not exceed INR 8 Lakh.
  • The candidates must have taken admission through the CAP process.

Education Concession to the Children of Ex-Servicemen, Maharashtra

  • The wards/widows/wife of Ex-Servicemen can apply.
  • They must be studying in Maharashtra only at a government-aided college.

Vocational Education Maintenance Allowance to ST candidates, Maharashtra

  • The candidates seeking vocational courses are eligible to apply.
  • They must have passed the previous examination.
  • The annual income of the family should be less than or equal to INR 2.5 Lakh.

Vocational Education Fee Reimbursement, Maharashtra

  • The candidates enrolled in vocational courses like Pharmacy, Engineering, MBA, MCA, Vaastu Shastra, etc.
  • The annual income of the family should not exceed INR 2.5 Lakh.

Jawaharlal Nehru University Scheme, Maharashtra

  • The candidates studying at graduation or postgraduation level can apply.
  • They must have taken admission to JNU.

Education Concession to Children of Freedom Fighter, Maharashtra

  • Wards/Widow/Wife of freedom fighters can apply for this scheme.
  • They must be studying in Maharashtra only.

Government Research Adhichatra, Maharashtra

  • The scholarship is available to postgraduate candidates.
  • They must be studying in Maharashtra only at one of the following institutions –
    • Govt. Science Institute (Mumbai, Nagpur, Aurangabad),
    • Govt. Vidarbha Gyan Vidnyan Science Institute (Amravati),
    • Vasant Rao Naik Mahavidyalaya College (Nagpur),
    • Universities and affiliated colleges
  • They must have obtained at least 60% marks in postgraduation.

Maintenance Allowance for SC candidates in Professional Courses, Maharashtra

  • The scholarship is available for candidates registered in professional courses.
  • The annual income of the family should be less than INR 2.5 Lakh from all sources.
  • The candidates must be living in a hostel and be a scholarship holder of the Government of India.

Maintenance Allowance to VJNT and SBC candidates in Professional Courses, Maharashtra

  • The candidates seeking professional courses like engineering, medical, animal husbandry, etc. can apply for the scholarship.
  • The annual income of the family should be less than or equal to INR 1 Lakh.
  • The applicant must be living in a government hostel connected to professional college.

Government Vidyaniketan Scholarship, Maharashtra

  • The scholarship is suitable for candidates who have done class 10 from Government Vidyaniketan only.
  • They must have qualified class 10 with 60% marks.

Tuition Fees and Examination Fees to ST candidates, Maharashtra

  • The candidates must have passed their previous examination.
  • The annual income of the family should not exceed INR 2.5 Lakh.

Post Matric Scholarship Scheme Government of India, Maharashtra

  • The candidates who have passed class 10 examinations can apply for this scholarship.
  • They must be studying at the post-matric level.
  • The annual income of the family should be less than or equal to INR 2.50 Lakh.

Tuition Fees and Examination Fees to OBC candidates, Maharashtra

  • The candidates studying in class 11 to postgraduation level can apply.
  • The annual income of the family should be less than or equal to INR 8 Lakh.

Government of India Post-Matric Scholarship for SC candidates, Maharashtra

  • The candidates who have passed class 10th can apply for this scholarship.
  • They must be seeking studies at the post-matric level.
  • The annual family income should be less than INR 2.50 Lakh.

Post Matric Scholarship to OBC candidates, Maharashtra

  • The candidates seeking studies at a post-matriculation level can apply.
  • The annual income of the family should be less than INR 1 Lakh.

Post-Matric Scholarship for Persons with Disability, Maharashtra

  • The disabled candidates of class 11 to postgraduation level can apply.
  • The level of disability should be 40% or more.

Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship, Maharashtra

  • The candidates belonging to the scheduled caste can apply.
  • There is no income limit for this scholarship.
  • The applicants must have passed the SSC examination with 75% or more marks and taken admission in class 11.

Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Shikshan Shulk Shishyavrutti Yojna, Maharashtra

  • The scholarship is applicable for candidates who have taken admission to a professional or non-professional course can apply.
  • Candidate must have taken admission under the general category.
  • The annual income of the family should be less than INR 8 Lakh.

Post-Matric Scholarship to VJNT candidates, Maharashtra

  • The candidates who are seeking studies at the post matriculation level can apply for this scholarship.
  • The annual income of the family should be less than INR 1 Lakh.
  • The candidates engaged in full-time employment or repeaters are not eligible to apply for the scholarship.

Chief Minister Fellowship (CMF) Program, Maharashtra

  • The candidates in the age group of 21 to 26 years can apply for this fellowship.
  • They must have graduated with a first-class.
  • They must have a minimum work experience of 1 year.
  • They must be proficient in the Marathi language.
  • They must be responsible, enterprising and enthusiastic students.

State Government Daxshina Adhichatra Scholarship, Maharashtra

  • The scholarship is applicable for graduate candidates from non-agricultural universities.
  • They must be studying in Maharashtra only.

Eklavya Scholarship, Maharashtra

  • The candidates who have completed their graduation in arts, science, law and commerce stream and have taken admission in a postgraduate program can apply for the scholarship.
  • The minimum percentage of marks to be obtained by them is 60% (for arts, commerce and law stream) and 70% (for science stream).
  • The annual income of the family should be less than INR 75,000 from all sources.
  • The candidates engaged in full-time or part-time employment cannot apply.

Post-Matric Tuition Fee and Examination Fee (Freeship), Maharashtra

  • The candidates who have passed SSC or equivalent matric are eligible for this scholarship.
    They must be studying in a government recognized institute located in Maharashtra.
  • The annual income of the family should be less than INR 2,50,000.

Maharashtra Scholarships Online Application Process

The Maharashtra government also runs its online Maharashtra scholarship portal namely, MahaDBT scholarship 2019-2020 portal by which the candidates can log in to MahaDBT login and apply for various scholarships. Know how to check Maharashtra Scholarship Status. Know the steps to be followed to apply for the scholarships through the MahaDBT portal.

Step 1: Online Registration

The candidates are available to both UID based registration and non-UID based registration.

For UID Based Registration

  • Go to the MahaDBT Scholarship website at https://mahadbtmahait.gov.in/
  • Click on “New Applicant Registration”.
  • Click on “Yes” and enter Aadhaar number to proceed.
  • Authenticate student’s Aadhaar details either by using OTP (through linked mobile number) or by using Biometric (if the mobile number is not linked with Aadhaar)
  • Once, the Aadhaar details are authenticated, fill in all the required details on the registration form.
  • Create candidates login details (username and password)
  • Verify the candidate’s email ID and mobile number via OTP for successful registration.
  • Save all details and proceed.

For Non-UID based registration

  • Visit the MahaDBT gov in Scholarship Website.
  • Click on “New Applicant Registration”.
  • Click on “No” and register either by using the Aadhaar enrolment ID or by knowing the current status of candidates Aadhaar number.
  • Fill in all the required details.
  • Verify the candidate’s email ID and mobile number through OTP for successful registration.
  • Create candidates login details (username and password)
  • Upload candidates photograph as specified and other supporting documents (identity proof, address proof and birth proof.
  • Click on “Register” to complete registration.

Step – 2: User Login Procedure

  • Once successfully registered, students can log in to the MahaDBT scholarship portal by clicking on “Applicant login”.
  • Enter candidates registered username and password to log in.
  • In case, students forget candidates’ password, students can click on the “Forget password” button to retrieve it.

Step – 3: Submission of Application Form

  • Once students have successfully logged in, students will be directed to the user dashboard.
  • Click on “Profile” to update the candidates’ profile.
  • If the candidate’s profile is 100% complete, students will get the list of suggested eligible scholarship schemes.
  • Select the scholarship student wants to apply for.
  • Click on the “Apply” button to proceed with the scholarship application.
  • Provide all necessary information and upload supporting documents (if required).
  • Finally, submit the application.

Step – 4: Checking Application Status

  • After successful submission of the scholarship application, the candidates can check the status of their application by clicking on “Applied Schemes”.
  • If the candidate’s application is under scrutiny, it will be visible under the head of “Under Scrutiny Applications”.
  • If the candidate’s application is approved, it will be visible under the head of “Approved Applications”.
  • If the candidate’s application is rejected, it will be visible under the head of “Rejected Applications”.
