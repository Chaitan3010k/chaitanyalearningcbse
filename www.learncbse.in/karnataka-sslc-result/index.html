Karnataka SSLC Result to be Released Today @ 12:00 PM | KSEEB 10th Board Result @ karresults.nic.in

May 12, 2019 by Bhagya

Karnataka SSLC Result 2019: Karnataka Class 10th Result will be announced by Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board (KSEEB) on its official website, http://karresults.nic.in/. The date for the release of results is expected to release today i.e. 30th April. however, there is no official announcement made yet by the Government of Karnataka. Students who have given the class SSC exams, the results will be declared for them by the conducting body, very soon.

KSEEB: As per KSEEB, the Karnataka Class 10 Results will be declared in the first week of May 2019.

Candidates who want to check Karnataka Board SSLC results have to enter their Roll Number as mentioned in their Admit Card. The exam for Karnataka board SSLC academic class was conducted from 21st March to 4th April 2019. As soon as the results will be announced by the Karnataka Board, we will be updating here regarding the same. Till that time students who are waiting desperately for the results, are requested to keep patience.

Students, who have appeared for Karnataka SSC exams should calculate approximate marks as per exam, given by them. Take out your questions paper, and start mentioning marks as per the correct answers and do the grand total for each subject. This will help you compare the marks obtained by you in the final results. If there is a notable difference between your expected marks and the original marks, they can apply for rechecking/re-evaluation.

Here in this article, we are going to give complete details for results of Karnataka SSLC result conducted this year in 2019. Keep reading with us.

Karnataka Board SSLC Result 2019

The results for Karnataka SSLC 2019 exams, will be released in the first week of May 2019, as per estimation is done with respect to SSLC result 2018 Karnataka. Let us give here a general description of the Karnataka Board SSLC exam.

  • Examination Name – Karnataka SSLC Exam 2019
  • Conducting Body – Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board(KSEEB)
  • Examination Mode – Offline(Pen and Paper)
  • Exam Dates – 21st March to 4th April 2019
  • Exam Results  – To be announced soon
  • Result Website – www.karresults.nic.in/

Karnataka SSLC Results 2019 – Steps to Check

Karnataka Board Result 2019 for SSLC will be declared on the official website and through online mode only. Later, students can collect their SSLC mark sheet from their respective Karnataka Board affiliated schools Below here are the steps to check the results through online mode, for SSC class exam conducted this year in 2019.

  • Firstly, the candidate has to visit the official Karnataka board official results website, www.karresults.nic.in.
  • Click on the link given for Karnataka Board SSLC results in 2019.
  • A new webpage will get opened where you will be asked to enter the registration number.
  • Enter the number and click on submit button.
  • The result of SSLC Karnataka board exam will be displayed on your screen.
  • Download the result for your reference.

Marksheet of Karnataka Board SSLC class consists of following details

  • Registration Number
  • Medium of instruction
  • Name of the Student
  • School Name and Code
  • Father’s and Mother’s name
  • Marks obtained in each subject
  • Total Marks Scored
  • Grade as per marks obtained
  • Class Obtained
  • Signature of school Principal

Karnataka SSLC Class Determination

Students who have appeared for the Karnataka Board SSLC exam in 2019, have to score 30% in each subject and 35% in overall subjects to pass the examination. The class for the results is determined with respect to below-given criteria’s.

Percentage Class
50% – 59.99% Second Class
60% – 84.99% First Class
85% and above Distinction

Karnataka SSLC Results – Re-evaluation

If the students are not satisfied with their marks or if they have not passed the exam, they can choose for revaluation of their results, declared by the Karnataka board or KSEEB. To apply for it, students will have to download the application form for a re-evaluation of marks available in the Karnataka Board online website.

Students have to send the filled scrutiny form along with a nominal fee deposit fee to the Karnataka Board authority. After the submission of the form, the Karnataka board personnel will recheck the answer sheets for the subject, which has to be re-evaluated or the objection has been raised. The cross-checked results will be published again on the official Karnataka Board results site.

Some Important Facts For Karnataka SSLC Results

  • The results will be announced by Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board(KSEEB), Government of Karnataka.
  • Students can check the result through online mode, in the official results website.
  • Student’s registration number is required to check the results.
  • Students have to pass in all the subjects, to get SSC certified.
  • Students can go for the re-evaluation of any subject if they are not satisfied with the results.
  • After the Karnataka SSLC results are declared, students can reach to their respective schools to get the mark sheet.

Karnataka PUC Result 2019 Announced

Karnataka Board conducts the exam for PUC or Class 12th as well. Its also named as Intermediate 2nd-year exam. The exam for this year was conducted on 1st March to 18th March 2019. The results for PUC-2nd year is declared on the official results website of Karnataka on 15th April 2019. Student’s have to follow the same steps as per mentioned for SSLC, to check the results. For the re-evaluation of results, please follow the same procedure as per mentioned for Karnataka SSLC exam.

About KSEEB

KSEEB, Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board was established in the year 1966. The aim of the Board is to restructure the operations of Secondary and PUC Education in Karnataka. The main objective is to conduct the board exam for SSLC classes and for PUC 1st year and 2nd year. Also, to prescribe syllabus for all the schools, private or government, which are affiliated to the board. The board is responsible to affiliate the private and government schools in Karnataka, also.

Karnataka board is responsible for the improvement of secondary education so that it meets the required needs and standards of economic and social development. KSEEB provides adequate facilities for the educational and professional advancement of socially and educationally backward communities.
