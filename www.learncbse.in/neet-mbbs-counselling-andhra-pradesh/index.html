NEET MBBS Counselling Andhra Pradesh 2019 | Detailed Counselling Procedure, Dates, Reservation Criteria & Seat Availability

July 14, 2019 by Bhagya

NEET MBBS Counselling Andhra Pradesh: Dr. NTR University of Health Sciences, Vijayawada will conduct  NEET MBBS Counselling for AP, 85% of state quota seats in medical and dental colleges. For NEET MBBS Counselling students must register in online, online registration has been started on June 22, 2019. those students who are interested and eligible can fill application form till June 28, 2019. Dr. NTR University has released AP NEET Eligible Merit list with names who are eligible to apply for MBBS AP NEET Counseling Procedure. These students are those who have completed class 12 in AP and passed in NEET UG 2019 Exam. on basis of this NEET Merit List will not be prepared, In order to get your name in NEET AP Provisional rank list, students must have to fill an application form and submit their NEET Scores by visiting the official website @ ntruhs.ap.gov.in.

NEET MBBS Counselling AP 2019 – Eligibility Criteria

Students must have secured equal or more than NTA NEET cutoff marks 2019. students who have score cutoff marks are eligible to NEET MBBS Counselling AP. Detailed NEET MBBS Counselling eligibility criteria is as follows:

  • Candidates should be an Indian National or Person of Indian Origin (PIO) / Overseas Citizens of India (OCI) Card Holder satisfying the local and non-local status of Andhra Pradesh.
  • Students must have passed Intermediate (10+2) examination with a minimum of 50% for OC, 40% for BC/SC/ST and 45% for OC PH category candidates.
  • Students must have completed 17 years of age as on 31-12-2019. Which means candidates born after 02-01-2003 are not eligible for admission into MBBS, BDS Courses.
  • Candidates should have secured the minimum NEET UG 2019 Cutoff marks.
  • Candidates should also be a domicile of Andhra Pradesh and must fulfill the local and non-local rules for admissions in 85% state quota seats.

NEET MBBS Counselling AP 2019 – Application Form

Students who have eligible and interested to take admissions in MBBS, BDS Courses in government and private colleges should apply by entering their merit position. Students must fill an application form on the official website of Dr. NTR University between 22/06/2019 to 28/06/2019. The application fee for NEET MBBS Andhra Pradesh Counselling is Rs. 3540/- for OC & BC candidates and Rs.2950/- for SC/ST candidates should be paid online through Debit/ Credit/ Net banking. The fee once paid will not be refunded at any circumstances.

Step by Step procedure to apply

Step – 1: Visit the official website of NTRUHS @ apmedadm.ntruhs.ap.gov.in.

Step – 2: Now Click on “Admissions for AP MBBS/BDS/BAMS/BHMS/BNYS/ BUMS Competent Authority Quota 2019”

Step – 3: Now on screen, you will get a page which shows register or login

Step – 4: If students do not register before then click on Candidate Registration, while registering students must enter their NEET Roll number, NEET rank, date of birth and mobile number. After successful registration students must receive a registration number and secure pin.

Step – 5: If the students already registered must click on “Candidate login”, now enter NEET Roll number, registration number (generated) and secure pin to log in.

Step – 6: After login, students must enter their class 10 and class 12 marks, NEET rank, and marks permanent address to verify the status of local and non-local.

Step – 7: After entering all details click on save and pay, now applicants should pay an application fee.

Step – 8: Make the payment by choosing online payment options.

Step – 9: After the payment is done, the filled application form will be displayed on the screen.

Step – 10: Now download the Application form and keep it for future reference.

NEET MBBS Counselling AP 2019 – Provisional & Final Merit List

NEET MBBS AP Provisional Merit List: Students who applied for Dr. NTR University state quota seats will be enlisted in the Provisional merit list based on their scores. Those students whose names are there in the provisional merit list will have to attend for document verification to check their details they furnished while filling the application form. Only original documents should be taken for document verification at the centers notified by the university.

NEET MBBS AP Final Merit List: Post verification of the original documents, the university will release the final merit list comprising the names of candidates eligible for seat allotment. Once the seat allotment procedure is completed candidates will be notified through seat allotment result. Students are requested to visit the official website as well for the latest updates.

NEET MBBS Andhra Pradesh Counselling 2019 – Document Verification

Students should be taken the following documents to the verification center for document verification:

  • Admit Card of NEET UG – 2019
  • Rank Card of NEET UG – 2019
  • SSC or equivalent examination showing the Date of Birth
  • Memorandum of marks of qualifying examination in Intermediate or Equivalent examination.
  • Transfer certificate
  • Study Certificate(s) from 6th class to Intermediate.
  • In case candidates studied in the institutions outside the state of Andhra Pradesh, a residence certificate of the candidate or either of the parent issued by MRO / Tahsildar.
  • In case candidates claiming eligibility of reservation under Special categories should furnish the required certificate in support of their claim
  • Permanent Caste Certificate claiming reservation under BC/SC/ST Categories
  • In case claiming fee exemption, the latest Income certificate of the Parent issued by MRO / Tahsildar or white ration card.
  • Aadhar Card
  • Minority certificate

If all the above-mentioned documents are verified and valid, those students name must be in Final NEET MBBS AP Merit list 2019. on the basis of final merit list, seat allotment procedure will take place.
