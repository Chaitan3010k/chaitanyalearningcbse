MP Scholarship Eligibility Criteria

One of the major eligibility requirements that apply to all the scholarships is that they all are suitable for students who are the domicile of Madhya Pradesh. Apart from this, there are various other situations as well that you need to fulfill.

सभी छात्रवृत्तियों पर लागू होने वाली प्रमुख पात्रता आवश्यकताओं में से एक यह है कि वे सभी छात्रों के लिए उपयुक्त हैं जो मध्य प्रदेश के अधिवास हैं। इसके अलावा, कई अन्य परिस्थितियां भी हैं जिन्हें आपको पूरा करने की आवश्यकता है।

Mukhyamantri Medhavi Vidyarthi Yojana (MMVY), Madhya Pradesh

  • The students who have passed class 12th with 75% or more from the Secondary Education Board of Madhya Pradesh or 85% or more from CBSE/ICSE board.
  • The annual income limit of the family is INR 6 Lakh from all sources.
  • The students should be registered at an undergraduate level of course at a recognized institution or university.
  • The engineering candidates are required to have qualified JEE Mains examination with rank up to 50,000 or less.
  • The medical candidates are required to have got admission to a government/private medical or dental college through NEET merit.
  • The law candidates are required to have passed the CLAT examination and got admission to an NLU.

Mukhya Mantri Jan Kalyan Yojana (MMJKY), Madhya Pradesh

  • This scholarship is available for the children of people who are mentioned as the unorganized workman by the Labour Department of Madhya Pradesh.
  • The scholars must have got admission to an undergraduate, postgraduate, polytechnic, diploma or ITI program.
  • The engineering candidates are required to have passed the JEE Mains examination with a rank of less than 1,50,000.
  • The medical candidates are required to have got admission in the MBBS course via JIPMER, AIIMS entrance examination.
  • The law candidates are required to have got admission at NLUs by getting passing the CLAT examination.
  • The scholarship is also suitable for students who have obtained admission at government colleges in any undergraduate or integrated postgraduate course or dual degree course.

Post Matric Scholarship Scheme for OBC Students, Madhya Pradesh

  • The scholars pertaining to Other Backward Classes (OBC) who are studying at a post-secondary level can claim for the scholarship.
  • The annual family income should be less than INR 75,000 (for 100% scholarship).
  • The annual family income should be up to INR 1,00,000 (for 50% scholarship).

Post Matric Scholarship Scheme for SC Students, Madhya Pradesh

  • The students belonging to the SC category, studying at the post-secondary level can apply for the scholarship.

Post Matric Scholarship Scheme for ST Students, Madhya Pradesh

  • The students who belong to the ST category who are studying at the post-secondary level can apply for the scholarship.
  • The annual family income should be less than INR 2,50,000 (for 100% scholarship).
  • The annual family income should be between INR 2,50,000 and INR 6,00,000 (for 50% scholarship).

Model Higher Secondary Schools and Girls Education Complex Scholarship Scheme, Madhya Pradesh

  • The students who have passed class 5th, 8th, or 10th examination can apply.
  • The percentage of marks obtained by them in the final examination should be more than 60%

How To Apply for MP Scholarship?

To make it easy, the Government of Madhya Pradesh provides an online state scholarship portal mp to proceed with the scholarship application process, for the students of MP. Each student has to register on the portal to apply for the scholarship of their choice. Below are the steps to complete the MP Scholarship Registration through the MP scholarship Portal 2019.

  • Visit the official portal of State MP Scholarship Portal 2.0 Login, Madhya Pradesh.

  • Now, scroll down to get the column of Student Corner and click on ‘Register Yourself’.
  • Fill in the applicant’s details and submit it to complete the registration.
  • Upon registering, a username and password will be generated.
  • Now, go to Online Schemes on the MP Scholarship Portal Login and select the scholarship to be applied.
  • Scroll down and click on ‘If Registered, Login Here’.
  • Now, log into the MP scholarship Portal 2.0 using the generated username and password.
  • Fill in all the necessary details and upload the required documents.
  • Submit the scholarship application form.

  • सरकार द्वारा प्रस्तावित सभी छात्रवृत्ति और कल्याणकारी योजनाएं एमपी छात्रवृत्ति पोर्टल में प्रस्तुत की जाती हैं।
  • प्रवेश योजनाओं के लिए परामर्श के बारे में विशेषताएं, चल रही योजनाओं से संबंधित महत्वपूर्ण नोटिस और कई अन्य संबंधित विवरण एमपी छात्रवृत्ति पोर्टल पर पाए जाते हैं।

FAQ’s on MP Scholarship

Question 1.
What is MP Scholarship Portal?

Answer:
MP Scholarship Portal is an online scholarship gateway, run by the Government of Madhya Pradesh. Also stated as MP Scholarship 2.0, it is a one-stop online platform where MP students can find all the scholarships that are available for SC, ST, OBC and other categories.

Question 2.
What is the list of MP scholarships?

Answer:
The list of Scholarships provided by Madhya Pradesh government are:

  • Mukhyamantri Medhavi Vidyarthi Yojana (MMVY)
  • Mukhya Mantri Jan Kalyan Yojana (MMJKY)
  • Post Matric Scholarship Scheme for OBC Students
  • Post Matric Scholarship Scheme for SC Students
  • Post Matric Scholarship Scheme for ST Students
  • Model Higher Secondary Schools and Girls Education Complex Scholarship Scheme

Question 3.
Whether I have to fill up the online application in one sitting?

Answer:
No. You can register by entering your necessary details. The system would generate an Applicant-id & password for you. Please note down the generated Applicant-Id & Password. Later you can fill up the online application in as many sittings as you wish until you are satisfied that you have entered all desirable fields correctly. The software provides the facility to save your application. Once you “Lock” your application, you cannot be able to modify it further.

Question 4.
How to check the status of my application?

Answer:
You can check the status of your application anytime. After logging in, click on “ Edit-Lock-Print Application”. Then select the “Track Status” option. This will provide you with the current / latest status of your Scholarship Application.

You can also find more Scholarship Articles for 12th passed, 10th passed Students and many more.
