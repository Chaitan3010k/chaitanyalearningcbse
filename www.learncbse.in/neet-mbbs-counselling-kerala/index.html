NEET MBBS Counselling Kerala 2019 | Detailed Counselling Procedure, Dates, Number of Seats & More

September 2, 2019 by Bhagya

NEET MBBS Counselling Kerala 2019: NEET MBBS Counselling Kerala 2019 admission procedure of state quota seats are organized by the commissioner of Entrance Examinations (CEE), Thiruvananthapuram. NEET MBBS Counselling Kerala will have three rounds in online mode for candidates whose name are there in NEET Merit List 2019. Students must register themselves on the official website of CEE to participate in the state merit list, students must register in between 16 – 21 July 2019. Those students who have qualified NEET 2019 and registered in time by submitting the application form are able to attend Kerala NEET MBBS Counselling 2019. Based upon state merit list and choices filled by students and state reservation criteria CEE will grant admissions to students of an approximate 3182 MBBS and 2077 BDS Seats under 85% of state quota seats.

NEET Counselling

Check NEET MBBS Kerala Rank List (Released)

Here in this article, we will provide all the necessary information about NEET MBBS Counselling Kerala 2019, how to register, seat allotment procedure, documents required and list of top government and private MBBS colleges in Kerala. Read on to find more about NEET MBBS Counselling Kerala 2019.

NEET MBBS Counselling Kerala 2019

NEET MBBS Counselling Kerala requires students to fill an application form on or before June 21 by submitting their NEET Result 2019 to CEE. Based Upon the submitted NEET Result to CEE, CEE will prepare a merit list. The first phase of counseling will start after the Merit List is released. NEET MBBS Counselling Kerala 2019 Login

Students who wish to appear in CEE Kerala Counselling must enroll in medical allied Courses of KEAM. For enrolling, students must have registered previously in KEAM 2019 which was closed in the march of March. After the completion of registration students will receive Application Number and Password. Students need to save these Application Number and Password for future login @ cee.kerala.gov.in.

NEET MBBS Counselling Kerala 2019 – Submission of NEET Result

All existing students must have credentials like KEAM 2019 (Application Number and Password) and NEET 2019 (Roll Number, Application Number, and Date of Birth) ready for the submission. Follow the following steps to submit NEET-UG result to the state committee, CEE:

  • Step – 1: Visit the official website of www.cee.kerala.gov.in.
  • Step – 2: Now Click on the link KEAM 2019 – Candidate Portal link.
  • Step – 3: In respective fields enter KEAM Application Number and Password along with the captcha.
  • Step – 4: After the completion of successful login into KEAM Candidate Portal, Click on the NEET Result Submission 2019 from the menu.
  • Step – 5: Now Provide NEET (UG)-2019 Roll Number, NEET (UG)-2019 Application Number and KEAM-2019 Date of Birth
  • Step – 6: Now submit after entering all the necessary NEET Details.
  • Step – 7: On Screen, it will display NEET result details such as Photograph, Name, NEET (UG)-2019 score, NEET Percentile Score, NEET All India Rank, etc. Verify them carefully.
  • Step – 8: All the details displayed on the screen match your NEET Score, now click on the Verified and Submit button.
  • Step – 9: At last take a print out of the NEET Result Submission report and keep it safe for future reference.

NEET MBBS Counselling Kerala 2019 – Choice Filling and Locking

Based on the submission of NEET Scores during the Kerala MBBS application process, CEE, Kerala will release the rank list. Kerala MBBS Rank list will have the name of the candidate, state medical rank, NEET Roll No., NEET Rank, NEET score & percentile scores. Students with names on the state merit list should apply for a Centralized CAP by visiting the official website (login).

After successful login students will be able to view a list of medical and dental colleges as per the state rank. The list will also show the number of seats per particular college. Candidates must follow the choice filling instructions described and add the institutes they choose in the preference box. Once the colleges are chosen, candidates can click on the submit button. Students will not be able to make any changes furtherly once the choices have been submitted in the portal. CEE, Kerala will also conduct trial counseling sessions to provide an idea on the whole counseling procedure in that college.

NEET MBBS Counselling Kerala 2019 – Seat Allotment Process Result

Based on the Kerala Merit List, preferences of the candidates, availability of seats and seat reservation criteria, CEE will allocate the medical and dental seats for NEET 2019 candidates. NEET MBBS Kerala Counselling first phase will be released in online mode in the first week of July 2019. Candidates can check their result for first phase NEET MBBS Counselling Kerala by logging in on the KEAM website. Based on the result students must report to colleges they have opted to complete the seat allotment procedure. The seat allotment result for Round 1 will be released in the first week of August 2019 based on the performance of the candidate at the reporting centre.

NEET MBBS Counselling Kerala 2019 – Reporting at the Centre

Students whose names on the State Counselling Merit List must report at the counseling centre with the following documents

  • Nativity certificate
  • Class 10 or SSLC certificate as a proof for birth date
  • Self-attested copy of Class 12 mark sheet or original course attested by the head of the institution last attended.
  • Self-attested copies of HSC or equivalent examination mark sheet
  • NCL certificate (for SEBC/OEC reservation)
  • Community certificate (for SC/ST aspirants)
  • Original certificates for those claiming special reservation
  • Self-attested medical certificate (for PH candidates)
