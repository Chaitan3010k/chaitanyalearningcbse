New CBSE Date Sheet for Class 10 and Class 12 (Released) | Download CBSE Time Table from Here

May 18, 2020 by Veerendra

CBSE Date Sheet 2020: The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) authorities will release the date sheet for CBSE 12 and Class 10 2020 for the board examinations that will be held for the academic session 2019 – 2020. According to the CBSE class 12th time table 2020, Class 12 board exams from February to April and Class 10 board exams from February to March. To give students sufficient time between consecutive papers, CBSE has released the board exam time table seven weeks before the exam date. CBSE Exam Date 2020 class 10 will begin in the month of  March 2020. CBSE Exam Date 2020 class 12 will begin in the month of March 2020. Get the 12th and 10th Board Exam 2020 date sheet from this article.

Latest Update:

CBSE date sheet 2020 for the pending exams has been released by HRD Minister. CBSE 10th exams for northeast Delhi will be conducted from July 1, July 2, July 10. CBSE 12th exams will be conducted from July 1 to July 15th.
