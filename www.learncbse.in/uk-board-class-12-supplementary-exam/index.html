UK Board Class 12 Supplementary Exam 2019 | Notification (Released), Time Table, Admit Card, Results

June 25, 2019 by Bhagya

Uttarakhand Board Class 12 Supplementary Exam 2019: Fondly known as the UK Board, the Uttarakhand Board of School Education (UBSE) was incepted as the Uttaranchal ShikshaEvamParikshaParishad in the year 2001. In 2008, it was renamed as the Uttarakhand Board of School Education (UBSE) in the year 2008. Being the sole School-level board, UBSE has been entrusted the responsibility of looking after and managing all the school level education in the state of Uttarakhand. The Board’s responsibilities also include the conduct of annual board exam for secondary and higher secondary students and prescribing syllabus to over 10,000 affiliated schools. Every year, the UK Board Class 10 and Class12 Board Exams are taken by nearly 3 lakh candidates at around 1300 examination centers.

State Board Supplementary Exams

The results for the main board exams were released on 30th May 2019. If you haven’t passed them, don’t worry, you get another chance! The supplementary exams are to be held soon for the class 12th failed students. In the meantime, you need to register and download your admit card. Read on to find out more.

UK Board Class 12 Supplementary Exam 2019 Overview

The Uttarakhand Board class 12 Supplementary exam is held after the main board exams for the students who couldn’t pass the latter. It is an offline subjective exam, for a duration of 3 hours. The syllabus will be same as that of the main board exams and is prescribed by the Uttarakhand Board. For more details, visit the official website @ ubse.uk.gov.in

Name of Board Uttarakhand Board of School Education
Category Uttarakhand 12th Supplementary
Exam Date (Tentative) June 2019
Result Date (Tentative) July 2019
Website ubse.uk.gov.in.

Uttarakhand Board Class 12 Supplementary Exam Registration 2019

After the main board exams, you van check your results on the official website (Released on May 30, 2019). If you haven’t passed in one or more examination, you need to register for each of those subjects on the website. The application form is to be released tentatively in the month of June. The registration is just a few easy steps

  1. Visit the official website (link given above).
  2. On the left hand side of the homepage, there is a “Registration” section. Click on it.
  3. Click on the “Intermediate Supplementary Registration 2019” link.
  4. Enter all the relevant details limke your old roll number etc.
  5. Download a copy of the registration form for future reference.

Go through the form a few times before submitting to make sure that there aren’t any discrepancies. Also make sure that you have applied for all the courses in which you didn’t pass the main board exams.

Uttarakhand Board Class 12 Supplementary Exam Admit Card 2019

The admit card is a very important document without which you won’t be allowed to attempt the exam. You will be able to download your admit card only after you reigster for the Uttarakhand Board class 12 Supplementary exams. (Check the steps given above if you haven’t registered yet). Take a few extra copies of the admit card to be safe. Don;t forget to take it with you to the exam hall. To download the admit card, follow these easy steps:

  1. Visit the official website (link given above).
  2. On the left hand side of the homepage, there is a “Results/Admit cards” section. Click on it.
  3. Click on the “12th Supplementary Admit Card 2019” link.
  4. Log in using your credentials.
  5. You will now be able to view the admit card. Download it and take a print out of it immediately.

Make sure to check the admit card for any discrepancies/errors. If any are found, notify the concerned authorities immediately.

Uttarakhand Board Class 12 Supplementary Exam Date Sheet 2019

To reduce the stress associated with the exams a bit, you need to prepare well. You have to make full use of the time available to you in studying, answering previous year papers and going through the mistakes you made in the main board exmas to ensure that you don’t repeat them. There are very few days available for preparation and every moment is precious. You need to fully understand the syllabus and exam pattern (the past year papers will help in this) and make sure that you focus on your weaknesses. For a fully efficient study plan, you need to know the exam time table. It would be quite useless to prepare for the last exam first and not leave enough time for the first exam. Hence, download the time table/date sheet as soon as it is released by following these steps:

  1. Visit the official website (link given above).
  2. On the left hand side of the homepage, there is a “Examination Scheme” section. Click on it.
  3. Click on the “12th Supplementary Date sheet 2019” link.
  4. You will now be able to view the date sheet. Download it and take a print out of it for convenience.

Note that the time table could change at any point and hence it is wiser to get the date sheet only from the official website, as it will reflect all the latest changes made on it. Keep yourself updated with the notfication published on the website to make sure that you don’t miss out on anything.

Uttarakhand Board Class 12 Supplementary Exam Results 2019

The results will be released on the main website after the supplementary exams end. You can check the results in online mode by logging in using your credentials. This is when all the hardwork you made pays off. The results will be released tentatively in the month of August. In the anxiety and excitement of checking the results, any delay might frustrate you. Hence, to avoid any hassles, follow the steps given here to get the results:

  1. Visit the official website (link given above).
  2. On the left hand side of the homepage, there is a “Results/Admit cards” section. Click on it.
  3. Click on the “Intermediate compartment result 2019” link.
  4. Log in using your credentials.
  5. You will now be able to view your results. Download it and take a print out of it for future reference.

Your result/score card will mention these following details

  • Candidate Name
  • Registration/Roll Number
  • Name of the Examination
  • Name of the Board
  • Parent’s Name
  • Previous Marks Obtained
  • New Marks obtained
  • Total Marks
  • Instructions

We hope that this article helped you understand more about the Uttarakhand Board class 12 Supplementary exam 2019. The registration process is very simple and if you follow the steps given here, will be done in a few clicks. Don’t forget to download the admit card after you register. You won’t able to take part in the exmas without it. The results, to be released in the monoth of August tentatively, can also be found on the official websute by following the given steps.

Do not panic, and study properly for the exams. Do check out other study-related articles to have your doubts cleared. Good luck!
