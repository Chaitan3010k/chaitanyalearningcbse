NEET 2020: What is NEET 2020? National Eligibility Cum Entrance Test (NEET) is a national level medical entrance examination for admission into the MBBS, BDS courses. NTA is responsible to conduct the NEET examination and this year NEET 2020 exam will be held on 3rd May 2020 through offline mode.

The NEET 2020 application form will be released on the 1st week of November 2020 and the last date to submit the NEET 2020 application form was on 31st December 2020. So the aspirants who wanted to secure a Medical Seat must appear for the NEET exam and get qualifying marks in the examination. The NEET 2020 exam will be going to commence from 3rd May 2020 in pen paper-based mode.

NEET exam is also considered to be one of the toughest entrance examinations in India and also this year about 12 lakh candidates are registered for the NEET 2019 examination. So the candidates who are appearing for NEET 2020 exam must be aware of everything about  NEET 2020 exam dates, syllabus, exam pattern, etc., In this article, we will provide you with all the necessary information regarding NEET 2020. Read on to know everything about NEET 2020.

All those candidates who have passed class 12 are eligible to appear for the National Eligibility Entrance Test (NEET UG 2019). The exam will be held in the pen-and-paper mode and from March 27, the applicants can download the NEET admit card. The result for NEET is scheduled to release on June 4.

NEET Eligibility Criteria

The eligibility criteria for NEET 2020 are listed below:

  • Nationality: Indian Nationals, NRIs, OCIs, PIOs & Foreign Nationals.
  • Educational Qualification: Students must have opted for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology or Biotechnology in Class 11 & 12 from a recognized board or university.

How To Prepare for NEET 2020?

NEET 2020 is a national level competitive entrance examination for admission into medical courses across the university/institutes in India. So it is important for the students appearing for the NEET exam to schedule themselves with preparation plan to ace the NEET 2020 exams easily.

  • Know Your Syllabus: The first step towards the NEET preparation is to know your syllabus. Knowing your syllabus helps you to analyze how much to study for the examination.
  • Schedule A NEET 2020 Study Time Table: Schedule yourself with a study time table according to the syllabus. Also, include break time in the schedule and try to follow the study time table strictly.
  • Start From Basics: Always start from the basics. Starting from basics helps you to understand each and every concept clearly.
  • Make Notes: Not only studying or memorizing, but candidates are also advised to take notes of important points and the concepts. Also taking notes helps you in last-minute revisions.
  • Revise Regularly: It is important to revise the chapters whichever you read previously. Revising the chapters whichever you read regularly will help to crack the examination easily.
  • Take Mock Tests: Working on mock tests is really important as it helps you to analyze your preparation level. Also, it helps you to improve time management skills and test-taking strategy.
  • Solve Previous Year Papers: Solve previous year papers. Solving previous year NEET papers helps you to analyze how the questions will be asked in the examination and how to face them.

Formula To Calculate NEET 2020 Marks

Follow the steps as below to calculate NEET 2020 Marks:

  • For every correct answer, 4 marks are awarded.
  • For marking incorrect option, -1 is deducted.
  • No mark is deducted for un-attempted questions and marking more than 1 option.
  • Total Marks = 180 MCQs X 4 = 720 Marks

So the NEET Score = (Total no of correct answers X4) – (Total No of Incorrect answers X1)
